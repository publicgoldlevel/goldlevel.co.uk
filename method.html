Method

A public method for turning ambiguity into a usable next step.

The public version of the method is deliberately simple: diagnose, structure, publish, apply.

1

Diagnose

Read the live material closely enough to identify the main issue and what is making the situation unclear.

2

Structure

Translate the situation into clearer categories, hierarchy, and next-step logic.

3

Publish

Return the result as a readable page, brief, packet, or bounded written diagnostic.

4

Apply

Use the result to support a decision, revision, release, or another defined move.

Why it works

Clarity over sprawl.

The public method stays readable because it aims for cleaner judgment and a usable next move rather than maximal theory.
