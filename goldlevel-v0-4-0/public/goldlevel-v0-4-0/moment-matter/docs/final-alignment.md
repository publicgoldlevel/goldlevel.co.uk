# Final Alignment

The final site is not a card shop alone and not a broad diagnostic system. It is a visual translation studio.

The public surface is mainstream: cards, custom pieces, digital files, physical prints, boxed sets, clarity maps, and art for spaces.

The deeper skill remains intact: pattern recognition, relational/systemic reading, visual explanation, iterative creation, and turning complexity into usable artifacts.

The commerce route is practical from day one: digital delivery worldwide, physical formats with shipping estimates, and an order-request checkout that can be connected to live payments later.
