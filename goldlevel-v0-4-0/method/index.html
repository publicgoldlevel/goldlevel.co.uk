Ten-layer stack
LayerFunctionWebsite implementation
I. Surface ExpressionPublic-facing form layer: visible objects/elements, signals carried, available patterns, visible outcomes.Reads what the market can see before any claim about internals is made.
II. Field LayerContext, environment, latent possibility space, boundary conditions.Defines the containing frame so the asset is not diagnosed in abstraction.
III. Selection LayerNoise-to-signal, governed inclusion/exclusion, framing, attention gate.Controls what enters the map and what remains outside current scope.
IV. Operator LayerSelection → transformation → interpretation → responsibility → proof demand.Defines the functional operator without ego inflation and assigns consequence.
V. Invariant ChannelsLeading symmetry, constraints, invariant channels, conservation, residue/structure.Names what remains stable across versions, attempts, silence and objections.
VI. Radar / CalibrationCalibration, confidence, false-signal detection, pattern strength, uncertainty.Prevents aesthetic coherence from being confused with market proof.
VII. Basin Topology ActivationBasin depth, transient paths, recurrent core, multi-basin ecology, attraction pull.Maps the landscape of pull, stability and flow.
VIII. Governance and ConstraintsPermission gates, ethics, trajectory, constraint memory, pause, repair, termination.Decides what can continue, what must slow, and what must stop.
IX. Consequence and Cost LayerTime, attention, repair, opportunity, reputational and compounding costs.Makes every path pay its cost before escalation.
X. Proof and State PromotionReceipts, tests, artifacts, measured results, state promotion; no promotion without proof.Moves interpretation into validated state only when evidence exists.
