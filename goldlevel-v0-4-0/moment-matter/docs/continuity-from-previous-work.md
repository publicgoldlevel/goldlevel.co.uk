# Continuity From Previous Work

The previous service architecture is not deleted. It has been translated.

## Previously broad framing

The earlier site contained decision reads, diagnostic reads, input/return logic, scope, boundary, good-fit checks, service routes, and packet-style outputs.

## New public translation

- “Diagnostic read” becomes Clarity Map.
- “Input and return” becomes send the moment / receive the piece.
- “Boundary” becomes clear scope and a defined output.
- “Good fit” becomes use this when you need clarity, not more content.
- “Packages” become digital, physical, custom, and space formats.
- “System” becomes the hidden production method rather than the public sales language.

## Reason for the shift

The new business begins with something people can understand and buy: a card, print, custom piece, or clarity map. The deeper method remains present as the engine behind the work.
