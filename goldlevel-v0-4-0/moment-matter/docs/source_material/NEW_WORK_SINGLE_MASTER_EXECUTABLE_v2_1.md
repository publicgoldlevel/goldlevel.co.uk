## GLOBAL IMAGE PROMPT HEADER — COPY/PASTE

```text
Create one vertical 4:5 cinematic fine-art image for a coherent visual sequence about inner essence becoming receivable form, and return becoming allowed.

The work is born through the author, but it is not owned by the author. The audience enters through its own lens. Money is one form of return, not the meaning of the work.

Express this through material relation, distance, proportion, residue, threshold, silence, and atmosphere.

Use a restrained museum-grade visual language: dark earth, black water, aged paper, graphite, worn cloth, clay, glass, shadow, horizon, muted brass, bone white, warm grey, quiet interiors, and one subtle gold or warm-light trace only if necessary.

Do not make the image religious, occult, cosmic, angelic, diagrammatic, or symbolic in a direct explanatory way. The emotional layer must be felt before it is visually understood.

Image-only. No readable text, no stage numbers, no captions, no labels, no logos, no watermarks.
```
