Proof / receipt discipline

Claims stay bounded until receipts exist.

The read can identify clarity, trust, route, and action issues. It does not guarantee sales, legal outcomes, financial outcomes, medical outcomes, search ranking, platform approval, or public validation.

Outputs can become proof only when supported by saved files, change logs, screenshots, deployment logs, payment/customer receipts, or user-confirmed facts.
