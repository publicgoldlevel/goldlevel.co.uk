# Business Redesign — Public Premise

## Core public sentence

I create custom visual pieces, cards, prints, and clarity maps for moments that are hard to explain.

## What people can buy

- A card when words are hard.
- A custom visual piece for a specific life moment.
- A Moment Map for a relationship, transition, decision, apology, grief, or new chapter.
- A Clarity Map for a creative pivot, business direction, or relationship pattern.
- Digital pieces for worldwide delivery and local printing.
- Physical pieces for gifting, framing, and premium presentation.
- Curated pieces for professional spaces.

## Mainstream language rule

The public site avoids internal project language and uses simple commercial wording: custom pieces, meaningful cards, visual maps, thoughtful gifts, calm spaces, digital delivery, physical prints, worldwide shipping.

## Spark of uniqueness

The unique idea is not “prints” by itself. It is visual translation: taking a situation that feels layered, hard to say, or difficult to place, and turning it into an artifact that can be kept, gifted, printed, framed, or acted from.
