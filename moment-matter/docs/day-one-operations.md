# Day-One Operations

## Digital

Digital products can be delivered worldwide by email as PDFs, image files, written notes, or print-ready assets. This is the fastest route and the lowest-friction international offer.

## Physical

Physical products include printed cards, prints, boxed sets, and professional space sets. These are priced higher than digital formats because they require production, packaging, handling, quality checks, and shipping preparation.

## Worldwide shipping

The static checkout estimates UK and worldwide postage. The order request can then be confirmed manually before production or connected to a live payment provider.

## Payment path

The current archive uses order-request checkout. It is appropriate for a day-one launch because it avoids overbuilding and still lets the business accept serious enquiries, quote physical shipping, and send a payment link or invoice.
