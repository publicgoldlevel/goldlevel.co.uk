# Lived Alchemy — ChatGPT Image Creator Prompt Pack

**Subtitle:** How experience becomes knowledge, and knowledge becomes form.

## How to use this pack in ChatGPT Image Creator

Use this as a **sequence system**, not as one overloaded image prompt.

1. Start with `00_MASTER_SEQUENCE_IMAGE_CREATOR.md` to establish the visual language.
2. For each stage, open the matching numbered `.md` file.
3. Copy the **Image Creator Prompt** into ChatGPT Image Creator.
4. Generate one stage at a time.
5. Keep the same aspect ratio across all stages. Recommended: **vertical 4:5** for Instagram carousel.
6. Do **not** ask the image model to place long text inside the image. Add carousel text later in Canva, Figma, Photoshop, or the Instagram editor.
7. If a stage comes out too literal, regenerate with: “make it more symbolic, quieter, less illustrative, less crowded.”
8. If a stage comes out too mystical, regenerate with: “make it more grounded, material, editorial, and restrained.”

## Global Visual DNA

Create a coherent 12-image fine-art visual sequence called **Lived Alchemy**.

The sequence should feel:
- cinematic;
- elemental;
- manuscript-forward;
- restrained;
- symbolic but not obscure;
- grounded in matter, not fantasy;
- poetic without becoming decorative mysticism.

### Core transformation

raw → held → distilled → tested → mirrored → named → made → broken → revealed → refined → proportioned → settled

### Recurring motifs

Use these lightly across the full sequence:
- dark earth;
- black water;
- stone;
- manuscript fragments;
- fine gold thread or gold seam;
- glass;
- ash;
- clay;
- weather;
- horizon;
- quiet human presence.

### Palette

Restrained gold / earth / black / stone / water palette:
- charcoal black;
- dark soil;
- wet clay;
- oxidized metal;
- aged paper;
- bone white;
- muted umber;
- mineral grey;
- dusk blue;
- fine gold line or trace.

### Composition principles

- One dominant image per stage.
- Strong negative space.
- Avoid visual clutter.
- Mystery through image.
- Clarity through structure.
- No decorative occult symbolism.
- No motivational poster look.
- No literal diagrams unless the stage specifically requires study or structure.
- Avoid text in the generated image unless explicitly requested.

### Global Negative Prompt

Avoid: fantasy alchemy circles, glowing magic symbols, tarot aesthetics, generic wellness imagery, corporate stock photography, motivational poster design, obvious AI surrealism, neon colours, excessive gold, cliché spiritual iconography, clutter, readable text, logos, watermarks, melodrama, heroic poses, over-explained symbolism.

## Recommended ChatGPT Image Creator instruction format

Paste this before each stage prompt when generating:

> Create one vertical 4:5 cinematic fine-art image for a coherent visual sequence titled **Lived Alchemy**. Use the same restrained elemental visual language across the sequence: dark earth, black water, stone, aged manuscript, fine gold trace, ash, clay, glass, weather, horizon. Make the image symbolic, material, and museum-grade. Do not include readable text, logos, or watermarks.

Then paste the individual stage prompt.


## Stage Index

01. **The Raw Signal** — Experience enters before meaning.  
   Visual: Dark earth, fragments, rain, skin, buried gold.
02. **The Vessel** — Attention gives the signal a vessel.  
   Visual: Hands, black bowl, still water, protected flame.
03. **Distillation** — Reflection turns return into pattern.  
   Visual: Condensation, glass, gold droplets, hidden grammar.
04. **Testing** — Action tests what remains.  
   Visual: Gold strip, anvil, weather, precise force.
05. **Conversation** — The pattern meets other minds.  
   Visual: Partial figures, gold threads, shared air, reflective pool.
06. **Study** — Study gives instinct grammar.  
   Visual: Books, marginalia, diagrams, architecture of light.
07. **Creation** — Creation gives inner knowing a body.  
   Visual: Clay vessel, page, hand, seedling.
08. **Destruction** — Destruction shows what cannot carry the charge.  
   Visual: Cracked vessel, ash, compost, fine gold seam.
09. **Projection** — Projection reveals the lens.  
   Visual: Warped mirror pool, horizon, shadow, solitary figure.
10. **Refinement** — The knower shapes the knowing.  
   Visual: Tuning fork, compass, aperture, stone circles.
11. **Nature and Matter** — Nature teaches proportion. Matter teaches change.  
   Visual: Roots, tides, shell spiral, mineral veins, growth rings.
12. **Silence** — Silence lets the work become wisdom.  
   Visual: Still water, gold horizon, small figure, quiet ruin.

## Batch Generation Note

Generate images one at a time. Do not paste all 12 stage prompts into the image creator at once unless you are asking it to make a contact sheet or storyboard. For final art, each stage needs its own generation pass.
