**Core movement:** refined

**Visual anchor:** Tuning fork, compass, aperture, stone circles.

Create one vertical 4:5 cinematic fine-art image for a coherent visual sequence titled **Lived Alchemy**. Symbolic still life: vibrating tuning fork beside a compass, eye-like aperture, and concentric circles drawn into stone dust, subtle breath-like motion in the air, restrained metal stone black gold palette, disciplined refinement, no text.

Avoid occult eye symbolism, personality-test aesthetics, medical instruments, self-help icons, excessive geometry, bright sci-fi interface.
