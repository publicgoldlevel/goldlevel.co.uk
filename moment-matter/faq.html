FAQ

Common questions.

How the process works, what to expect, delivery, payment, and everything in between.

Custom pieces

How does a custom piece work?
You share the moment, situation, person, and tone using the brief builder or by emailing us directly. We translate that into a visual piece — a card, print, Moment Map, or Clarity Map — and deliver it by email (digital) or post (physical). One round of refinement is included.
How much information do you need in the brief?
Enough to understand the moment. A few sentences about who the piece is for, what's happening, and the tone you want is usually enough. If you're not sure what to include, use the brief builder — it guides you through the key points without making you write an essay.
What if the piece doesn't feel right?
One round of refinement is included in every custom piece. If something is off — the tone, a specific phrase, the direction — let us know and we'll adjust. The piece should land right. If it doesn't, we work until it does.
How long does a custom piece take?
Typical turnaround is 48 hours from confirmed brief and payment. We'll confirm the timeline when we confirm the order. If you need something sooner, mention it — we'll tell you honestly whether it's possible.

Delivery

How does digital delivery work?
Digital pieces are delivered by email as print-ready PDF files, phone-friendly image files, and any written notes. No postage. Delivered anywhere in the world. You can print locally, send digitally, or both.
How does physical delivery work?
Physical pieces — cards, prints, boxed sets — are produced, packaged, and shipped carefully. UK and worldwide shipping is available. The postage cost is estimated at checkout based on your destination. Physical prices include production and handling; postage is additional.
Can I ship anywhere in the world?
Yes. Digital delivery is worldwide at no postage cost. Physical shipping is available to the UK and internationally. Postage is estimated at checkout by destination.

Payment

How do I pay?
Product pages use secure Shopify checkout. Choose the format you want, complete checkout, and PayPal Express will appear there when available through Shopify. For custom work, we confirm the brief before production begins.
Do I pay before or after I see the piece?
Payment is completed before work begins. For custom pieces, your brief gives us the direction; we confirm any necessary details before production. One round of refinement is included in every custom piece.

Clarity Maps

What is a Clarity Map and who is it for?
A Clarity Map turns a live situation — a decision under pressure, a creative pivot, a relationship pattern, a business direction question — into a clear visual structure, written summary, and one next-step frame. It's for people who have the material but need the pattern made visible before they can act.
Is a Clarity Map therapeutic advice or business consulting?
No. A Clarity Map is a visual translation of a situation you describe. It is not therapy, legal advice, financial advice, medical advice, or consulting with guaranteed outcomes. It gives you a clear visual structure and next-step frame for your own decision-making.
