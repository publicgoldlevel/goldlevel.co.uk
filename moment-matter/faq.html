FAQ

Common questions.

How the four core offers work, what to send, delivery, payment, and refinements.

Custom work

What do you make?
The foundation range has four offers: a digital Moment Card, printed Moment Card, digital Moment Map, and printed Moment Map.
What is the difference between a card and a map?
A card is best for a message. A Moment Map is best when the whole situation needs a visual-and-word piece: a transition, decision, relationship moment, or milestone.
How much information do you need?
A few clear sentences are usually enough: who it is for, what is happening, the tone, and anything to include or avoid. The brief builder helps structure this.
Is one refinement included?
Yes. One refinement round is included for custom pieces so the tone, wording, or direction can be adjusted if needed.

Delivery

How does digital delivery work?
Digital pieces are delivered by email. They can be sent digitally, kept, or printed locally. There is no postage cost for digital delivery.
How does printed delivery work?
Printed pieces are produced, packaged, and posted. Physical pricing reflects custom work, production, handling, and packaging. Shipping is handled at checkout where available.
Can this be ordered from outside the UK?
Digital delivery is worldwide. Printed delivery depends on the shipping options available in checkout.

Payment and boundaries

How do I pay?
Product pages route through secure Shopify checkout. PayPal Express appears there when enabled through Shopify.
Why are the printed versions more expensive?
Printed pieces include the custom design work plus production, handling, packaging, and physical delivery preparation. Digital pieces avoid those costs.
Are Clarity Maps, boxed sets, and space sets still available?
They are not part of the current foundation range. The site is intentionally focused on four core offers first: card/map × digital/printed.
