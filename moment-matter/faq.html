FAQ

Questions before ordering.

Is this art or a service?

Both. Ready-made pieces can be bought like normal prints or digital files. Custom pieces include interpretation, wording, and visual creation.

Can I buy from outside the UK?

Yes. Digital formats are worldwide by default. Physical pieces can be prepared for UK or international shipping.

Are physical pieces more expensive?

Yes. Physical prices account for printing, packaging, handling, and preparation. Postage is estimated separately by destination.

Is this therapy or advice?

No. Clarity Maps help structure material and reflect patterns, but they do not replace professional legal, medical, financial, or therapeutic advice.

Can I print digital files locally?

Yes. Digital formats are designed for personal-use local printing.

How do I start?

Choose a format, add it to your basket, or use the custom brief builder to prepare a clear request.
