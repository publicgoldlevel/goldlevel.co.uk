FAQ

Common questions.

The current range is small: card or map, digital or printed.

The offers

What can I order?
Four offers: a digital Moment Card, printed Moment Card, digital Moment Map, and printed Moment Map.
What is the difference between a card and a map?
A card is best for a message. A Moment Map is best when the whole situation needs a visual-and-word piece: a transition, decision, relationship moment, or milestone.
What do I send after ordering?
Send who the piece is for, what is happening, the tone you want, and anything to include or avoid. A few clear sentences are usually enough.
Is one refinement included?
Yes. One refinement round is included for custom pieces so the tone, wording, or direction can be adjusted if needed.

Delivery

How does digital delivery work?
Digital pieces are delivered by email. They can be sent digitally, kept, or printed locally. There is no postage cost.
Why are printed versions more expensive?
Printed pieces include the custom work plus production, handling, packaging, and shipping preparation.

Payment

How do I pay?
Product pages route to secure Shopify checkout. PayPal Express appears there when enabled through Shopify.
Are the other product lines available?
Not in the current public range. The website is focused on the four core offers first.
