Getting started

What to send us about the room.

Helpful to include

  • Photos of the room (phone photos are fine)
  • The purpose of the room and what happens there
  • Approximate wall dimensions
  • The tone or atmosphere you want to create
  • Any colours, materials, or existing pieces already in the room
  • Whether you need a digital recommendation, physical pieces, or both
  • Budget range if you have one

What we'll return

  • A focused recommendation for artwork selection
  • Suggested placement and arrangement notes
  • Format and size guidance for the space
  • Next-step options (digital download, physical print order, or both)
  • A single point of contact throughout
