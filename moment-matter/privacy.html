Privacy

This static website does not collect form submissions.

There is no live intake form, basket database, account system, analytics requirement, or payment-data collection inside this static website package.

Product pages show static PayPal button placeholders. Once live PayPal buttons are added, payment information is handled by PayPal rather than by this website.

If you choose to email or message Moment & Matter, only send information that is relevant to creating the piece. Do not send unnecessary sensitive material.

Image assets may load from external image hosts used by the project. Those providers may receive ordinary technical request data when images are loaded by your browser.
