# Clarity Map — SignalNest AI

## Story basis

SignalNest AI has traffic, referrals, and partner-webinar momentum, but its homepage creates curiosity without qualified demo requests because buyers cannot tell what the product means for their role.

## Visual brief

Create a professional diagnostic-map image for a B2B SaaS homepage clarity case. Show a dark editorial workspace with a homepage wireframe, four labeled pressure nodes: role ownership, problem cost, trust boundary, safe next action. Visual language: restrained, premium, gold linework, no cartoon figures, no mystical symbolism, no dense readable copy. Aspect ratio 4:5.

## Must avoid

- fake client proof
- readable fabricated testimonials
- revenue charts implying actual results
- spiritual/mystical overclaiming
- cartoon mascot dependency
- over-dense text inside the image
