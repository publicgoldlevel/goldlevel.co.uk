How this works

Two columns. One decision.

Each packet has a free preview PDF and a full paid version. The preview gives you enough to decide. The full version gives you the complete working document.

01

Browse the list

Each row is one packet. The title and short description tell you what the packet covers and how long it is.

02

Download the preview

The free column links directly to a PDF preview hosted on GitHub. No sign-in required. Download and read before deciding.

03

Unlock the full version

The paid column links to the purchase route. After payment, the full PDF is sent to your email or Signal within 24 hours.
