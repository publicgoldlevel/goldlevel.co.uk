# GOLDLEVEL Offer Clarity 24h Execution Board

## Goal

Convert the live landing page from passive proof surface into one paid diagnostic read.

## Active offer

Live-But-Not-Landing Read — £35 founding read.

Input:
One live page, post, pitch, profile or offer.

Output:
Written diagnostic naming:
1. what the buyer is not understanding;
2. where trust is leaking;
3. what sentence, block, CTA or offer structure to change first;
4. the next action to test.

Delivery:
24–48h.

Boundary:
Diagnostic read, not full copywriting, legal, tax, medical, benefits, regulated advice, or full implementation.

## 24h task sequence

1. Confirm landing page live.
2. Send 30 specific DMs.
3. Use landing page only after interest.
4. Track replies.
5. Take payment before delivery.
6. Deliver one written read.
7. Request one-line feedback and permission to anonymise excerpt.
8. Log receipt.

## Do not do

- Do not rebuild the whole page.
- Do not explain the whole GOLDLEVEL architecture.
- Do not claim traction before payment/feedback exists.
- Do not chase after discomfort or no.
