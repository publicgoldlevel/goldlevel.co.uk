Privacy notice

This page explains, in common English, what the static front door collects, what it does not collect, how consent works here, and where the practical limits of a front-end page begin.

What this page collects

This site asks only for a short qualification note: name, email, broad support type, timing, budget readiness, and a brief current-situation summary. It is designed to keep the first step narrow.

  • No payment details are requested.
  • No attachments are requested.
  • No identity documents are requested.
  • No full case files, medical histories, or confidential records should be sent through the page.

What this page does with the information

In this static version, the form runs in your browser. The page does not send the form onward by itself. If you choose to continue after the form, you copy what you want to send and move into Signal or Proton Mail by choice.

  • The page is built to support data minimization.
  • The page is built to reduce accidental oversharing.
  • The page is built to make consent visible before onward contact.

Cookies, trackers, and local browser controls

This site does not load analytics trackers, marketing pixels, ad tags, or third-party chat widgets.

  • A short local cooldown value may be written in your browser to slow repeated automated use.
  • That local value is not intended to profile you.
  • The site is designed to work without advertising cookies.

Legal basis and plain-English position

The operational base is England, United Kingdom. The privacy position is drafted around transparency, minimization, purpose limitation, and user control in line with UK GDPR-style expectations and broader GDPR-style principles.

  • This page is a front-door qualification page, not a general data collection portal.
  • Consent here means consent to use the limited page for fit review and pre-contract contact.
  • If you continue into Signal or Proton Mail, the data handling position of that route also matters.
