AI Technological Adolescence

No. 03·visual systems map·760×1013

Summary

A map of AI as a system in technological adolescence: capable, fast-moving, powerful, and not yet fully mature in its public responsibilities.

What it maps

Immaturity, capability growth, public exposure, governance lag, institutional pressure, and the need for maturity before unchecked scaling.

Why it matters

The question is not whether AI can do more. The question is whether the operating environment has matured enough to carry what AI can now do.
