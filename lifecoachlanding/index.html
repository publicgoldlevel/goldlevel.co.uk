FAQ

Practical answers before the first page.

Do I need a full website?

Not necessarily. If you are still clarifying your offer, a focused landing page may be the better first step.

Can this replace a designer or agency?

No. This is a bounded page build and message-structure service, not a full agency engagement.

What if my offer is not clear yet?

That can be part of the first pass. The page can help clarify your message, but the offer still needs enough substance to structure.

Can you add booking or email capture?

Simple links can be included now. More advanced booking, email, analytics, or automation should be treated as an expansion stage unless separately agreed and tested.

Will this get me clients?

No result can be guaranteed. The purpose is to make your online presence clearer, more credible, and easier to act on.

What should I send first?

Send your current page if you have one, your coaching description, your offer notes, and the action you want visitors to take.
